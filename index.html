About Bohemian Life

Built around color, confidence, and creative freedom

Bohemian Life is a lifestyle brand celebrating color, creativity, and expressive living.

The brand is created by Aimee's Bohemian Jewelry & Imports LLC, a family-run business based in Knoxville, Tennessee. Since 2005, Aimee’s Bohemian has been known for eclectic clothing, jewelry, and unique gifts that reflect individuality and global inspiration.

Bohemian Life continues that spirit, bringing bold style and creative energy into a new collection designed for people who live freely and wear their personality proudly.
